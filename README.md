### About

I build systems that look simple on the surface and carry heavy lifting underneath. Four years in, currently leading a team of 10 engineers at Tekki Web Solutions, working across full stack, AI agents, DevOps, and large-scale scraping infrastructure.

My process starts on a whiteboard, moves fast into a validated prototype, and only then becomes production code, optimized for speed and accuracy once real usage tells me what matters.
